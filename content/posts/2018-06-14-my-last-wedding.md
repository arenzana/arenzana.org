It is always about her, isn&#8217;t it? It&#8217;s been a few years and just a couple of weeks ago I shot my final wedding. That smile, that first dance&#8230; they never get old. It&#8217;s been such a great ride. It has been truly amazing. I could have never imagined I would learn so much from something that started as a hobby.

Megan and Jake have been my last couple and I just don&#8217;t think I could&#8217;ve asked for more. They were funny, kind, and truly a great couple to work with. Going back to my first few weddings, I&#8217;ve noticed how much of a photojournalistic influence my style has acquired. It might be the lens election, but the occasion just called for it. I wanted to go with something bigger. I believe I succeeded in telling the story. A story of friendship.
